# Welcome

We are a small collection of creators and makers. Turning ideas into reality.

## COVID-19 Changes

Our previous mission was to provide a lot of hands on and in person assistance to our customers. In March 2020 the decision was made to "slow down" and nearly suspend  operations.  After nearly a year a new mission has been developed and we've set course in a new direction.

We no longer make "house calls." Previously we would come onsite to work hands on with customers. Now we are offering virtual meetings. 

### Additional Projects

We have some new projects in the works.  These projects do not align with our original mission however we intend to provide a broad range of services and actual products in the near future.
